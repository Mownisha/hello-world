HELLO WORLD

A program to greet the planet

This page is an attempt to greet everyone on Earth using our various human languages

We're interested to see how long our list can become

We'll start with languages that the epicodus staff could offer without using Google

  • English: Hello, world!
  • Spanish: Hola, mundo!
  • Japanese: Konnichiwa, sekai!
  • French: Bonjour, monde!
  • Kinyarwanda: Mwirwe, isi!
